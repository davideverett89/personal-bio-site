About Me

My interest in the web and web development began in the mid 90's when we got our first family computer and had access to the Internet for the first time. I was immediately fascinated by the nearly instant access to information on any topic my young mind could possibly be curious about. There was now a conduit for me to experience a little piece of whatever I wanted, and I could directly interact with it and immerse myself in it. I wanted to harness this magic for my own use. This interest carried on into the days of Myspace, where I would use HTML to try and spruce up my profile with band backgrounds, colors, animations, and GIFs in order to impress my friends. The jury's still out on how well that worked, and there was a lot of copy and paste going on, but the seed that had been planted many years earlier was now beginning to sprout. Web development was put aside for many years in order to pursue another passion of mine: music. I now find myself coming full circle back to web development. I've realized that the combination of creativity and problem solving that it requires is something that I crave, much like music. Also like music, I can use web developmemt to enhance the lives of others and this is ultimately what I hope to do.
