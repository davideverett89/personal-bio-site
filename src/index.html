About Me

I am a web developer and musician. My interest in web development began as a child in the mid 90's. Once I experienced the thrill of seeing my code render in the browser, I began to envision a day where I could build my own site. After pursuing music for many years, I now find myself drawn back to that old childhood dream. I find that music and web development are very similar in that they exist at the crossroads of analytic thinking and creativity. Both can also be used to enhance the lives of others and that is ultimately what I hope to do.
